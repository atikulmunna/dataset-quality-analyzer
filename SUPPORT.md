# Private alpha support

The hosted service is a selected-tester alpha maintained by Atikul Munna. It has no guaranteed response time, uptime, data recovery, or long-term availability.

For access, account removal, deletion help, or a failed audit, email `atikul.munna@northsouth.edu`. Include:

- the job ID shown in the dashboard;
- the UTC time of the problem;
- the visible status or bounded error code; and
- the browser and device type when the issue is in the interface.

Do not send passwords, access tokens, dataset files, or sensitive dataset contents by email. Security vulnerabilities should follow [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md), not the general support path.

The operator may pause new audits when alarms fire, the queue is unhealthy, or the project approaches its USD 50 total-spend ceiling. The local CLI remains the no-account, no-hosted-compute alternative.
